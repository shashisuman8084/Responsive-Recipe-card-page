Burger Recipe

About Burger recipe

A burger is a popular dish consisting of a cooked patty made from ground meat or a plant-based alternative, typically placed between a sliced bun and accompanied by various toppings and condiments.

The patty can be seasoned with ingredients like garlic, onion, salt, and spices for flavor, and is often cooked on a grill, skillet, or barbecue.

Common toppings include lettuce, tomato, cheese, onion, pickles, and sauces such as ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard.

Burgers can be customized with additional ingredients like bacon, avocado, sautéed mushrooms, or specialty sauces, making them a versatile and beloved meal option served with sides like fries or salads.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef (or chicken/turkey): 500g (1 lb)
  • Breadcrumbs: 1/4 cup (optional, for binding)
  • egg: 1 (optional, for binding)
  • Garlic: 1 clove, minced
  • Onion: 1 small, finely chopped or grated
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Black pepper: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Paprika or chili powder: 1/2 teaspoon (optional)
  • Worcestershire sauce: 1 teaspoon (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ground meat with garlic, onion, salt, pepper, paprika, and Worcestershire sauce (if using).
  2. Add breadcrumbs and an egg if needed to help bind the mixture.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties about 1/2 inch thick.
  4. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease with oil or butter.
  5. Cook the patties for 3–4 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked (internal temperature should reach 160°F/71°C for beef).
  6. If making cheeseburgers, place a cheese slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking and let it melt.
  7. Lightly butter the insides of the buns and toast them on the same pan or grill until golden brown.
  8. Spread ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard on the bottom bun.
  9. Layer with lettuce, a patty, a tomato slice, onion rings, and pickles.
  10. Cover with the top bun.
  11. Serve with fries, coleslaw, or your favorite sides. Enjoy your homemade burger!
