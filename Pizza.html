Pizza Recipe

About Pizza recipe

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy and has become a global favorite due to its versatility and delightful taste. It consists of a round, flat base of dough, topped with a variety of ingredients and baked to perfection.

A pizza recipe is like a blank canvas—you can personalize it with your favorite toppings, sauces, and cheeses, making it perfect for every taste and occasion.

From kids to adults, everyone loves pizza! It's perfect for parties, family dinners, or a cozy night in.

pizza, dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked quickly—usually, in a commercial setting, using a wood-fired oven heated to a very high temperature—and served hot.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 packet of yeast
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 cup of pizza sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
  2. Gradually add warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out the dough into a circle and place it on a pizza pan.
  7. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, followed by your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  10. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy!
