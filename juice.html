Basic Juice Ingredients:

    For Fruit Juice:

  • Fruits: Any of your choice, such as oranges, apples, mangoes, pineapples, or berries.
  • Water: As needed for consistency.
  • Sugar or Honey: Optional, for sweetness.
  • Lemon Juice: 1 teaspoon (optional, for tanginess).
  • Ice Cubes: Optional, for serving chilled.

    • For Vegetable Juice:

  • Vegetables: Examples include carrots, beets, celery, spinach, or cucumber.
  • Water: As needed for blending or juicing.
  • Black Pepper or Chaat Masala: Optional, for flavor.
  • Lemon Juice: 1 teaspoon (optional).

Instructions:

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a pot.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of tea leaves or 1 tea bag.
  3. Simmer for 2–3 minutes (add sugar, ginger, or spices if desired).
  4. Add 1/4–1/2 cup milk (optional) and boil for another minute.
  5. Strain into a cup and serve hot.
